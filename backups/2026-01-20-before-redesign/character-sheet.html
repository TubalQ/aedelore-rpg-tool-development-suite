Aedelore Quick Reference

Core Dice System (D10)

Dice Pool: Each ability point = 1D10

Dice Allocation:

  • 1-2 points: 1 die
  • 3-4 points: 2 dice
  • 5-6 points: 3 dice
  • 6-7 points: 4 dice
  • 8-9 points: 5 dice
  • 10+ points: 6 dice

Maximum 8 dice per action unless a special class ability allows exceeding this limit.

Success Levels
  • 1-5: Failure
  • 6-7: Barely a success (minimal effect)
  • 8-9: Success (intended goal achieved)
  • 10: Critical success (exceeds expectations; roll again for possible bonus)
Combat

Initiative: Roll D6 to determine turn order (D10 if 6+ players)

Attack Roll: Core ability + Skill + Weapon attack bonus (Max 8 dice)

  • 0 successes: Miss
  • 1 success: Glancing blow (50% damage)
  • 2+ successes: Full damage

Damage: Roll 1D10 + weapon damage bonus

Defense Options:

  • Dodging: Dexterity roll
  • Parrying: Strength + weapon
  • Blocking: Strength + shield
  • Taking the hit: Strength + armor to reduce damage
Abilities & Spells

Roll 1D10 per ability point. At least one success required to activate.

Melee Classes (Warrior, Thief, Hunter, Outcast):

  • Use abilities with Weakened cost

Magic Classes (Mage, Druid):

  • Cast spells with Arcana cost
Arcana (Magic Resource)

Maximum Arcana:

  • Mage: Max 20, starts at 10
  • Druid: Max 16, starts at 8

Regeneration:

  • +1 Arcana per combat round
  • +2 Arcana per rest
Exhaustion & Bleeding

Weakened (Exhaustion): Characters have 6 weakened points.

When exhausted, roll 1D10 + Toughness:

  • 1-2: Death
  • 3-5: Fainting
  • 6-10: Can move but cannot fight

Bleeding: Roll 1D6 per turn:

  • 1-2: Death
  • 3-4: Lose 2 HP
  • 5-6: Lose 1 HP
Assisting Another Player

A character may forfeit their turn to grant +1 additional D10 to another player's related action, with DM approval.

Character Creation

Starting Points:

  • Attributes: 20 points to distribute
  • Skills: 10 points to distribute

Starting HP: Based on class and Toughness attribute

Starting Equipment: Varies by class

How to Use This Character Sheet

Local Saving

Your character data is saved locally in two ways:

  • Auto-save: Every 30 seconds, your data is automatically saved to your browser's localStorage
  • Manual save: Click "Save Local" to save immediately to your browser

Local saves are stored in your browser only - clearing cookies/cache will delete them!

Server Saving (Recommended)

Create an account to save your characters on the server. This allows you to access your characters from any device!

How to use:

  1. Click "Login" and register a new account (or login if you have one)
  2. Click "Save" (cloud) to save your current character
  3. Click "Load" (cloud) to see and load your saved characters

What gets saved to the server:

  • Character name, player name, race, class, and religion
  • All attributes (Strength, Agility, Intelligence, etc.)
  • HP, Willpower, Armor Class, and combat stats
  • All equipped weapons and armor (including broken status)
  • All spells and abilities
  • Consumables (potions, rations, torches, etc.)
  • Full inventory and notes/reminders
  • Backstory and character description

Server saves are safe from browser data loss and accessible from any device!

Local Data Loss Warning

Clearing browser cookies/cache will delete LOCAL saves! Use server saving or export regularly using "Export" to backup your data.

Backup & Transfer

To backup your character or move it to another device:

  1. Click "Export" to download your character as a JSON file
  2. On another device/browser, click "Import" to load it
